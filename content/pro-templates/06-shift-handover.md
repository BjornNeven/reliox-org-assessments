## âœ… Best Practices Guide

### When to Conduct Handover
- **Timing**: 15-30 minutes before shift end
- **Never rush**: A poor handover costs hours of confusion
- **Both parties present**: Face-to-face, not notes alone

### Where to Conduct Handover
- **Control room** for initial briefing
- **Field walkdown** for critical equipment
- **Maintenance shop** for tool/parts review

### How Long Should It Take?
| Situation | Duration |
|-----------|----------|
| Normal operations | 15-20 min |
| Active breakdown/repair | 30-45 min |
| Major shutdown/startup | 45-60 min |

### Who Should Attend?
- **Required**: Outgoing lead tech + Incoming lead tech
- **Recommended**: All technicians on shift change
- **As needed**: Supervisor, Production lead, Safety rep

### The 3-Step Handover Method
1. **TELL** â†’ Verbal briefing of key issues
2. **SHOW** â†’ Physical walkdown of critical areas
3. **CONFIRM** â†’ Incoming repeats back understanding

## âš ï¸ Common Handover Failures & Prevention

| Failure Mode | Impact | Prevention |
|--------------|--------|------------|
| **No handover at all** | Critical info lost, repeated work | Make it mandatory policy |
| **Verbal only, no documentation** | "He said/she said" disputes | Always complete written form |
| **Rushing through** | Missed safety hazards | Block 20min minimum on schedule |
| **Only positive info shared** | Problems continue unreported | Ask: "What went wrong today?" |
| **No equipment walkdown** | Unaware of actual conditions | Make field check mandatory |
| **Incoming doesn't ask questions** | Assumes, doesn't verify | Train active listening habits |
| **Vague work order status** | Unknown job completion level | Be specific: "50% complete, waiting for bearing" |
| **Ignoring safety permits** | LOTO violations, injuries | Verify every active permit together |
| **Not involving supervisor** | No escalation path for issues | Brief supervisor on critical items |
| **Previous shift items forgotten** | Recurring problems never resolved | Use follow-up checkbox tracking |
